Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to interest people in our company and influence them to rent our rafts and kayaks or pay for a trip with an expert water rafting guide leading the way.

Audience

My target customers are people over 15 years old that are interested in water rafting. My target audience wants to be able to go on water rafting trips with an experienced guide. They will expect to find information about water rafting and our company on this website. Information includes things like, price, location, dates, etc.
